Overview
The Information Technology Department at D Y Patil College of Engineering is a vibrant community where students are encouraged to explore, innovate, and excel. We foster a supportive and inclusive environment that values diversity and teamwork. We are committed to nurturing IT professionals who not only possess technical expertise but also have the skills to adapt, evolve, and contribute to the ever-changing IT landscape.

Department Activities

Second Year Induction 2022

Students come with diverse thoughts, backgrounds and preparations. It is important to assist them to adjust in the new environment and inculcate in them the ethos of the department with a sense of larger purpose. Its purpose is to make the students feel comfortable in their new environment, set a healthy daily routine, create bonding in the batch as well as between faculty, develop awareness, sensitivity and understanding of the self, society at large, and nature.

Sambhav Tech Fest 2022

Get ready to ignite your passion for technology at Sambhav, the ultimate college tech fest! With a perfect blend of innovation, competition, and excitement, Sambhav brings together bright minds to showcase their skills and explore the possibilities of the digital world. Experience captivating workshops, thrilling competitions, insightful guest lectures, and mesmerizing tech displays. Join us for an electrifying extravaganza that celebrates the power of technology and unleashes endless possibilities.

Jigyasa - Roadmaps for IT 2022

Embark on a journey of exploration and knowledge at Jigyasa, the enlightening IT event! Designed to guide you through the vast landscape of IT domains, Jigyasa offers comprehensive roadmaps to navigate your career path. Delve into interactive sessions, expert panels, and engaging workshops led by industry professionals. Uncover the intricacies of software development, data science, cybersecurity, AI, and more. Whether you're a beginner or an aspiring specialist, Ignite your passion for IT at Jigyasa!
